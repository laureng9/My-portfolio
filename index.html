About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! Having spent seven fulfilling years as an educator, zI embarked on a transformative journey during Covid-19 lockdown in 2020 when I discovered a newfound interest in coding. As the world slowed down, my curiosity for technology accelerated, leading me to immerse myself in the fascinating realm of coding.

While balancing part-time teaching responsibilities, 2022 marked the beginning of my formal coding education through Codecademy. Fast forward to July 2023, I made the pivotal decision to bid farewell to teaching and fully commit to the world of coding. This portfolio serves as a testament to my transition, capturing the fusion of my extensive teaching background with the evolving skills and passion I've cultivated on my coding odyssey.

Join me as I demonstrate the harmonious blend of knowledge and technology in this thrilling phase of my professional journey.
